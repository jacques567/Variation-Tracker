What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website or use a web application. They allow the service to remember your session so you don't have to re-authenticate on every page.

Variation Tracker uses only cookies that are necessary to operate the service. We do not use advertising cookies, behavioural tracking cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

Cookies we use

CookieProviderPurposeDuration
sb-* (auth session) Supabase Maintains your authenticated session so you remain logged in. Without this cookie the service cannot function. Session / up to 7 days
__stripe_mid, __stripe_sid Stripe Set by Stripe during payment checkout. Used to detect fraud and ensure secure payment processing. Up to 1 year
__cf_bm Cloudflare (via Stripe) Bot management cookie set during payment processing to distinguish human users from bots. 30 minutes

Cookies on the public signing page

The variation signing page (accessed by clients via a shareable link) does not require login and does not set any cookies. No tracking occurs on this page beyond the recording of the signature itself, which is disclosed in the privacy notice shown on that page.

Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking the Supabase session cookie will prevent you from logging in, as it is strictly necessary for authentication.
